TIKKA BIRYANI

RECIPY

Biryani (/bɜːrˈjɑːni/) is a mixed rice dish that was thought to have originated from Iran or South India. It is made with rice, some type of meat (chicken, beef, goat, lamb, prawn, or fish) and spices. To cater to vegetarians, in some cases, it is prepared by substituting vegetables for the meat.
